Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

TaskSU packs powerful organization features into a clean, distraction-free interface.

Smart Task Management

Create, edit, complete, and delete tasks with ease. Inline editing means you never lose your flow.

Priority Levels

Mark tasks as Low, Medium, or High priority. Color-coded tags make it instantly clear what needs attention first.

Due Dates & Overdue Tracking

Set due dates on any task. TaskSU automatically flags overdue tasks in red and counts them in your stats bar.

Task Categories

Organize tasks into Work, Study, Personal, Health, Finance, or Other. Each category has its own distinct color tag.

Real-Time Search

Find any task instantly. The search bar filters your task list as you type — no waiting, no page reload.

Dark & Light Theme

Switch between Dark, Light, and System themes. Your preference is saved and applied across all pages automatically.

Smart Filters

Filter tasks by All, Pending, Completed, High Priority, Overdue, or by Category. The overdue filter badge shows the count at a glance.

Live Stats Dashboard

A live stats bar always shows your Total, Pending, Completed, and Overdue task counts — updating instantly as you work.

Fully Responsive

TaskSU works beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Manage your tasks from any device, anywhere.
