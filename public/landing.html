Features

Nothing you don't.

TaskSU packs powerful organization features into a clean, distraction-free interface.

Smart Task Management

Create, edit, complete, and delete tasks with ease. Inline editing means you never lose your flow.

Priority Levels

Mark tasks as Low, Medium, or High priority. Color-coded tags make it instantly clear what needs attention.

Due Dates & Overdue Tracking

Set due dates on any task. TaskSU automatically flags overdue tasks in red and counts them in your stats.

Task Categories

Organize into Work, Study, Personal, Health, Finance, or Other. Each category has its own color tag.

Real-Time Search

Find any task instantly. The search bar filters your list as you type — no waiting, no page reload.

Dark, Light & System Theme

Switch between Dark, Light, and System themes. Your preference is saved and applied across all pages.

Smart Filters

Filter by All, Pending, Completed, High Priority, Overdue, or Category. The overdue badge shows the count.

Live Stats Dashboard

A live stats bar always shows Total, Pending, Completed, and Overdue counts — updating instantly as you work.

Profile Management

Update your username, change your password, or delete your account from the profile page at any time.

About

Built by a developer.
For everyone.

TaskSU was built by Simeon A. Tarr, a self-taught fullstack developer from Liberia 🇱🇷.

It started as a learning project — a way to master Node.js, Express, SQLite, and vanilla JavaScript by building something real. It grew into a fully deployed, production-ready web application.

The goal was simple: build a task manager that is fast, clean, and genuinely useful — without the bloat of enterprise tools. TaskSU is the result.
